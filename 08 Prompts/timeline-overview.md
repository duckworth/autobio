# Timeline Overview Prompt

Goal:

- turn a broad life-overview transcript into a starter chronology
- preserve uncertainty instead of forcing exact dates
- identify the biggest missing periods that need future recordings

Suggested instruction:

```text
Turn this transcript into a starter life timeline. Extract the major life periods, moves, schools, jobs, relationships, crises, recoveries, and turning points in approximate order. Keep uncertainty visible when dates or sequence are unclear. Then list the biggest timeline gaps that would benefit from future voice memos.
```
