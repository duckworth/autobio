# Cleanup Prompt

Goal:

- lightly clean the transcript
- preserve the speaker's voice
- do not invent facts
- keep uncertainty explicit when memory is fuzzy

Suggested instruction:

```text
Lightly clean this memoir transcript for readability while preserving voice, uncertainty, and factual ambiguity. Do not invent missing details, dates, motivations, or dialogue. Keep the transcript grounded in what was actually said.
```
