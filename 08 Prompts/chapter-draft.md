# Chapter Draft Prompt

Goal:

- turn selected transcript clusters into memoir prose
- preserve voice and uncertainty
- avoid made-up dialogue or invented scenes

Suggested instruction:

```text
Turn these selected memoir transcripts into a chapter fragment. Preserve the narrator's voice and uncertainty. Do not invent dialogue, chronology, interior thoughts, or sensory details that are not supported by the notes. Where memory is unclear, write that uncertainty into the prose instead of smoothing it over.
```
