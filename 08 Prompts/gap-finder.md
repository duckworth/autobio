# Gap Finder Prompt

Goal:

- find missing connective tissue
- surface unanswered questions
- suggest future recordings that would strengthen the story
- convert fuzzy concerns into concrete follow-up prompts

Suggested instruction:

```text
Review these notes and identify the biggest factual, chronological, and narrative gaps.

For each gap, output:
- `Gap:`
- `Why it matters:`
- `What is known already:`
- `What is uncertain:`
- `Follow-up recording prompt:`
- `Priority: high / medium / low`

Focus on gaps involving chronology, relationships, scene-level detail, turning points, motives, consequences, and transitions between life periods.

Do not guess at answers. If something may remain unknowable, say that clearly instead of trying to close the gap.
```
