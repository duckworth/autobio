## Status Guide

- `candidate`: promising but not yet organized
- `researching`: collecting supporting notes and filling gaps
- `drafting`: actively turning notes into prose
- `revising`: draft exists and is being reshaped
- `parked`: good material but not current priority

## Selection Rule

Do not promote a chapter to `drafting` until the key chronology, people, and scene gaps are at least mostly understood.
