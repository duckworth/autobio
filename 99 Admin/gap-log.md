## Status Guide

- `open`: identified but not yet scheduled
- `record-next`: high-priority follow-up memo needed
- `partially-filled`: some evidence exists but key details are still missing
- `resolved`: enough material exists to move forward

## Quality Rule

When you add a gap, always write the next recording prompt as something you could actually speak into Voice Memos in one sitting.
